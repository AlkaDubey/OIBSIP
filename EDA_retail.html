Objective

Perform exploratory data analysis on retail sales data to uncover sales trends, customer behavior, and product performance.

Key Findings

  • Clothing and Beauty categories generate the highest total revenue.
  • Female customers aged 2535 are the biggest spenders.
  • Sales peak in festival months (OctDec).
  • Quantity and Total Amount are highly correlated (expected for sales data).

Recommendations

  • Focus marketing on female customers aged 2535.
  • Stock top-selling products during high-demand months.
  • Offer loyalty rewards for repeat high-value customers.
